About Me

  Click here for my current Technical Resume or my full Chronological Resume.

  Hi, my name is Nicole. I recently completed my studies for Video Game Design and Development at TriOS College in London, Ontario. I am highly motivated with experience in various engines, platforms, and programming languages such as HTML5 & CSS3, ActionScript, C++, C#, Java, JavaScript, and some PHP. I am proficient with graphic tools as well like Photoshop and 3D modelling programs. I have experience with MVC, OOP, different version control systems (i.e. SVN, GitHub, etc.), and agile/SCRUM development methods as well. I also have experience with conventional web design and development. I am an extremely fast learner with impeccable organization skills and attention to detail.

  Games have always been a passion in my life, from playing Donkey Kong Country on my SNES when I was younger or now on my emulator on my Alienware. But more than just playing the game, how they made that was my main curiosity. I've come to enjoy the actual programming the most, although, as a former art student, I still love creating and designing the artwork and graphics as well. Now, I am eager to start my career as a developer.
